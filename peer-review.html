Peer Review Process

1. Submission

Students submit research via online form.

2. Editorial Screening

Editors check formatting, originality, and topic relevance.

3. Peer Review

Two reviewers evaluate methodology, clarity, and evidence strength.

4. Revision

Authors revise based on feedback.

5. Publication

Accepted papers are published in journal archive.
