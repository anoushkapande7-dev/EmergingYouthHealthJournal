Peer Review Process

All submissions undergo structured academic review before publication.

1. Submission

Students submit research through the online submission form.

2. Editorial Screening

Editors evaluate topic relevance, formatting, and originality.

3. Peer Review

Two reviewers assess methodology, evidence quality, and clarity.

4. Revision

Authors revise manuscripts based on reviewer feedback.

5. Publication

Approved papers are published in the journal archive.
