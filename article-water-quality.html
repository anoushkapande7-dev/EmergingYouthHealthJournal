Water Quality Analysis of Public Sites in New Jersey

Student Research Article • Public Health

Abstract

This study evaluates chemical properties of drinking water collected from multiple public locations in New Jersey.

Methods

Samples were collected from five locations and analyzed using standardized water testing kits.

Results

All samples were within safe drinking limits, with minor variation in mineral content.

Conclusion

Public water quality was generally safe but showed regional differences that may warrant further monitoring.
