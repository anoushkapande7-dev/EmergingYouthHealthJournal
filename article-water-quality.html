Water Quality Analysis of Public Sites in New Jersey

Student Research Article • Public Health

Abstract

This study evaluates water quality across public locations in New Jersey using standardized testing methods.

Methods

Samples were collected from five sites and analyzed using chemical test kits.

Results

All samples were within safe drinking limits with minor variation in mineral content.

Conclusion

Public water quality is generally safe but shows regional variation.
